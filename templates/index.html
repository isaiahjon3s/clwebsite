What We Do

Specialized PCB design, start to finish

From the first block diagram to fabrication-ready files, we handle every stage — and coordinate with fab and assembly partners on your behalf.

  • LED Illumination Electronics Driver boards for LED microscope illuminators — halogen replacement and new designs for Zeiss, Nikon, Olympus, Leica, and others.
  • Microscope Control Boards Controller PCBs for stage motion, focus drives, filter wheels, and illumination switching.
  • Capacitive Transducer Circuits High-resolution capacitive sensing front ends for precision displacement and force measurement.
  • MEMS & Test Probe Interfaces Low-noise signal conditioning and interface PCBs for metal MEMS, cantilever probes, and micro-scale sensors.
  • DFM & Fabrication Support Design-for-manufacturing review, Gerber generation, and coordination with fab and assembly partners.

Drop-in precision, instrument by instrument

Whether it's a halogen-to-LED retrofit for a Zeiss stereo zoom or a capacitive transducer front end, we design to fit the exact mechanical and electrical constraints of your platform.
